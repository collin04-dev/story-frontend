3 Days Before

Well...I guess I don't quite know how to start this, but they said that writing a journal would help calm me down a little. Hmm...I don't quite know how writing a journal will help me cope with the idea of me leaving our home galaxy, but I guess it can't hurt. 3 Days, that's going to go by quick...but it's kinda exciting. To know that I am heading to the very stars I have looked at my entire life...to know that I will be up there among them...the dream I have had ever since I was a kid...I am about the achieve it....

It's pretty hard to sleep now...but I know I have to. Well I guess this writing is helping me calm down a little...

Day 4

The Earth is absolutely beautiful. It's amazing that I have seen so many pictures and videos of Earth by all the astronauts before me, and I still can't believe it. I am living right now and I can't believe it...To know that I have lived here my entire life thus far, and I still haven't seen all of it. I always thought it crazy being on a mountain and seeing miles around...but now...now I am seeing entire continents and oceans! Well...here soon we will be basically sling-shotting ourselves to get to our destination and that's when the long part begins. Goodbye Earth...Goodbye Home .

Day 237

Another Day...well...I think it's a day. It's wierd not having a sun setting and rising. Now we just look at some of the clocks we have to keep track of time. Just a little digital monitor with numbers on it basically runs your day...but we are all still getting by. We go through our routines...Ripley helps out will all the data logging, Cooper helps out with all the tech services, and Hudson...well he helps keep moral up when we all get bored. We should be reaching LV-426 (The name of our new home planet), in a few more months... but in all honesty...I like the silence out here. The view is something that can't be beat .

41 Days After

Just Another Day...I look back at this journal I wrote over the course of all this, and it's pretty amazing to me how much has changed. I look back at the first day I started this journal, and see just how nervous I was..and if writing this thing would really help. Now here I am...writing in this journal under a tree sitting on a hill...just like on Earth . The Endurance has served as our primary base of operations for some time now, but we are now exploring and going out even more . It's funny looking back at everything...now that I am among the very stars that I once used to look upon.
